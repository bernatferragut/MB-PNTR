GALAXIES PAINTER

GALAXIES PAINTER | MEDITATION TOOL | BERNAT FERRAGUT 2020
  • Use ONLY with Google Chrome
  • Download this .hex file to your Microbit
  • Use the PAIR MICROBIT button for pairing devices
    • The microbit 'flat' on one of your hands should put the brush at the center of the screen (x=0, y=0)
  • Use the Control Panel at the top of the canvas to modify the size and color of the variables
  • Press the Point checkbox to draw points, and adjust its parameters
  • Press the Line checkbox to draw lines, and adjust its parameters
  • Use the microbit x,y,z axis to draw on the canvas by rotating them
  • Draw your design with patience and love
  • Use the 'SAVE' button to save your design
  • Uncheck both, point and line, to reset the screen to starting position
  • For more information please visit: bernatferragut.com
